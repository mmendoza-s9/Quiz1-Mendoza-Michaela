Kimi ni Todoke


Kimi ni Todoke or in english, "From Me to You" is a love story about the main character Sawako Kuronuma. Sawako Kuronuma is shy, timid, smart, and has a caring heart.
However, people in her school avoid her because of her name and appearance looking like Sadako from The Ring.
She is usually alone with no one to talk to, until she meets a guy named Shota Kazehaya.
Kazehaya is kind and isn't afraid of her for what she looks like. Instead, he likes her for who she is. From there, she began to hang out with Kazehaya and make friends
like Ayane Yano, Chizuru Yoshida, and Ryu Sanada in the story.
